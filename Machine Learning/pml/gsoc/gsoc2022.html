Google Summer of Code 2022

This page summarizes the contributions made to the pyprobml and ssm-jax open source libraries as part of the Google Summer of Code during the summer of 2022.

The students were mentored by Kevin Murphy (Google Research), Nipun Batra (IIT Gandhinagar), and Scott Linderman (Stanford).
